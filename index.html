    Airdrop FAQ

    Essential answers to help you claim, secure, and monitor your Phantom rewards with confidence.

    How do I confirm my Phantom wallet is eligible?

    Connect your wallet through the verification flow, enable multisig recovery, and make sure you have at least one recent transaction on Solana within the last 30 days.

    When will the airdrop distribution start?

    Distribution opens in rolling windows every 24 hours. Claiming early secures a higher boost, but you can finalize the payout anytime before the window expires.

    Are there any network or gas fees deducted?

    A minor network fee is applied at settlement. All boosted rewards are calculated net of fees, so the amount you see in your claim screen is the amount you will receive.

    What if I miss my confirmation window?

    If the confirmation window closes, simply reconnect your wallet to request a new slot. Your previous allocation is not guaranteed, so confirm as soon as you receive the prompt.
